Loan Requirements:

  1. Two (2) full colour recent passport photographs.
  2. Valid ID: (Passport, Driving Permit,National ID, Voters’ Card, Employment ID accompanied by the letter of introduction from the employer or LC 1).
  3. Photograph of the placed security.
  4. Document proving ownership of security; we only accept land title and Car log book accompanied by a dully signed transfer forms.
  5. Security for the loan.
  6. Loan processing fee of 2.5% of the amount borrowed
  7. Income statement of at least (3) three months for salary earners.
  8. Credible guarantor.
